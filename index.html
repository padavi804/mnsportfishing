Your Captain

Captain Jamie Cotner has worked his way through the ranks of charter fishing in Duluth. He has developed a few techniques not used in the area, and his experimentation and innovation has brought success to his boat. He is a a teacher in the off season and he enjoys teaching kids to fish.

Preparing for Your Trip

Please bring: fishing license, warm clothing, rain gear, sunscreen, sunglasses, food/snacks, and beverages. We provide all fishing equipment and safety gear.


Please, no bananas on board.

You’ll be fishing from the "Aluminauti," a fully restored 32’ Marinette Express with enough room for up to 6 anglers at a time. It also includes a toilet and ice box.

Our target species vary by season. We primarily fish for Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, and Walleye.

Lake Superior weather can be unpredictable. We do our best to get out on the big lake, but your safety and comfort is our top priority. If the waves are too large, we alternate our fishing location to the St. Louis River Bay.

We accommodate up to 6 people per charter. For larger groups, please contact us to arrange multiple boats.

No experience necessary! We welcome anglers of all skill levels and provide all necessary instruction.

We depart from Canal Park in Duluth, MN. The dock is located next to the historic William A. Irvin and can be accessed from the parking lot behind Hoops Brewing.

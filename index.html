Your Captain

Captain Jamie Cotner has worked his way through the ranks of charter fishing in Duluth. He has developed a few techniques not used in the area, and his experimentation and innovation has brought success to his boat. He is a a teacher in the off season and he enjoys teaching kids to fish.

Preparing for Your Trip

Please bring: warm clothing, rain gear, sunscreen, sunglasses, food/snacks, and beverages. We provide all fishing equipment and safety gear.

Full refund with 7 days notice. Weather-related cancellations can be rescheduled or refunded at any time.

We accommodate up to 4 people per charter. For larger groups, please contact us to arrange multiple boats.

No experience necessary! We welcome anglers of all skill levels and provide all necessary instruction.

We depart from the McQuade Small Craft Harbor, located at 5501 Ryan Rd, Duluth, MN 55804.
